ABOUT

The Renfrew County Amateur Radio Club Inc. is the official Radio Amateurs of Canada affiliated club for Renfrew County in Ontario, Canada. We’re a community of radio enthusiasts dedicated to promoting amateur radio through on-air activity, technical learning, and local connection.

NET

We host a weekly net every Monday at 7:30 PM across all club repeaters—everyone is welcome to check in and join the conversation.

BREAKFAST

Each month, members gather for an informal breakfast meet-up in either Deep River or Pembroke—an easy way to connect face-to-face with fellow hams.

TECH NIGHT

We also run Tech Night, a Zoom call held twice a month on Thursday evenings, where members share projects, troubleshoot problems, and explore new ideas in amateur radio and electronics.

Curious about our roots? Check out the History or visit the Events page for upcoming activities and a library of past Tech Night recordings.

RCARC REPEATERS

The Renfrew County Amateur Radio Club is located in the County of Renfrew in the Province of Ontario, Canada. We own and maintain four 2 metre repeaters and one 70 cm repeater in the area:

Repeater Frequency Location Tone Packet Node Packet BBS Packet Freq. APRS APRS Freq. AllStar Node
VE3NRR 146.760 - Pembroke 114.8hz VE3NRR-7 (KA) VE3NRR-1 145.010 60398
VE3NRR 448.025 - Pembroke
VE3UCR 145.430 - Foymount VE3UCR-7 (KA) VE3UCR-1 145.010
VA3RBW 146.790 - Point Alexander VA3RBW-7 (KA) VA3RBW-1 145.010 VA3RBW-3 144.390
VA3ZRR 147.120 + Renfrew 114.8hz

VE3ZRR is hard linked to VE3NRR. The other repeaters can be linked with DTMF codes to VE3NRR.

VE3NRR VHF has an IRLP node (2520). Node Status: Online

Note that the IRLP can only be used on NRR VHF. It won't work through the links or the UHF.

VE3NRR VHF also has an AllStar Link Node: 60398, and an Echolink node: 212717.
