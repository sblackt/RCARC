Renfrew County Amateur Radio Club Meetings

Our membership is typically around 30 and we meet every two months in Petawawa.

General Meetings:

Normally the third Sunday of odd months, excluding July.

Time 13:00

Location - Petawawa Civic Centre Fundraising Lounge. which is on the 2nd floor. Enter by the rink doors then up the stairs to the left.

2024 meetings:

January 21

March 17

May 19

September 22 (4th Sun.)

November 17 (Annual General Meeting)

Hope to see you there.

Note the new meeting place. It is now in the Fundraising Lounge up the stairs to the left after the rink entrance in the Petawawa Civic Centre at 16 Civic Centre Road. Civic Centre Road runs off Petawawa Blvd. at Murphy Road. Turn left, on to the first street past Kelsey’s Bar and there will be parking on the right side past the playgrounds at the Civic Centre.
