Who we are.

The Renfrew County Amateur Radio Club is located in the County of Renfrew in the province of Ontario, Canada. We operate four 2-metre repeaters and one 70 cm repeater in the area:

VE3ZBC is hard linked to VE3NRR. The other repeaters can be linked with DTMF codes to VE3NRR.

Our membership hovers around 50 and we meet every two months in Pembroke.
