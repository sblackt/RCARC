Petawawa Family Fun Run 2010-Oct-17

A Fund Raiser

A non-competitive event: raised $2700.00 for "Renfrew County Active Living Projects".

This money will be used to improve the bike skills park at the Petawawa Civic Centre.

Our ARES group provided several safety checkpoints and a bicycle mobile for sweeping the course.
