Renfrew County ARES

NET INFO

  • When: each Wednesday evening at 9:00 PM EDT/EST.
  • Where: on the Pembroke repeater, VE3NRR (146.760 -), and linked repeaters.

  • Net preamble
    •

Net controllers are asked to link into the VE3ZRR (531), VA3RBW (571), VE3UCR (521) and IRLP node 9035 just prior to beginning the net.

Please read the reflector rules before using the new Ontario ARES reflector.

When possible, other repeaters throughout the Renfrew County are linked together to allow opportunity for our wide disbursement of membership to check in.

  • Hard-linked to VE3ZBC in Bissett Creek extending our coverage in the west to the edges of Mattawa
  • Soft-linked to VE3ZRR in the east, extending to the east of Arnprior
  • Soft-linked to VA3RBW in Pt. Alexander
  • Soft-linked to VE3UCR in Foymount, extending coverage south and into Algonquin Park

Our Pembroke IRLP node number is 2520, and operation is through a link radio between the VE3NRR repeater and a high speed DSL internet connection.

A special thank you is extended to the Renfrew County Amateur Radio Club (RCARC), the Haliburton County Amateur Radio Club for the use of the IRLP reflector, and the Petawawa Civic Centre for hosting the IRLP link.

Please join in the net and say hello.
