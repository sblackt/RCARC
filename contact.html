We’d love to hear from you. Here are the best ways to reach us.

Get on the Air

The easiest way to reach us: join our Monday night net at 7:30 PM on VE3NRR (146.760 MHz, −600, 100 Hz CTCSS). Just listen in, or give us a call — all are welcome.

Come for Breakfast

Monthly club breakfast. No agenda, no formalities — just radio people, good coffee, and great conversation. Check the events page for the next date.
